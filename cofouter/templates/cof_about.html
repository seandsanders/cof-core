Catastrophic Overview Failure

Catastrophic Overview Failure is the premier new-pilot friendly wormhole corporation.

What Does Catastrophic Overview Failure Do?

We take new and old pilots into the depths of Wormhole space to teach them the intricacies of living in a wormhole. From not talking in local to scanning a chain, we'll walk you through every step of the way. We mainly focus on PvP, but we also do PvE fleets and large wormhole battles from time to time!

We are an open and welcoming community of pirates, cut-throats, assassins, renegades and oxymorons who are constantly looking for fresh blood to keep our glorious welping machine of lossmails running. We like to keep an open and fun-oriented atmosphere in TeamSpeak and in-game, but we try to be serious when it matters. We try to improve and do well in fights while keeping the light-hearted atmosphere that we love. We are a small community, which means you can get to know people beyond just their voice.

Whether you are new to wormhole space or a veteran to it, whether you are looking to learn to PvP or an expert at it, COF is the place for you!

How Can I Join?

COF has no clear SP requirement to join. We do, however, expect our members to have an interest in, and (at least try to) participate in PvP content. To be effective in COF, you need to be able to fly frigates with covert ops cloaks (Asteros are fine) and/or be able to fly one of our armor doctrine ships (Battlecruisers/T2 Cruisers). Experience in scanning is useful but even if you don't have any, give it a shot and we can teach you all you need to know! Join the “catastrophic overview failure” public channel if you have questions, or apply now!
